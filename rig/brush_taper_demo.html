The stroke is modulated by a ramp (the control field), then blurred, jittered with turbulence noise, and hard-thresholded back to opaque ink — so near the boundary it thins and breaks into dry-brush flecks, and past it, it's gone. Not an opacity fade: the output is solid ink.
