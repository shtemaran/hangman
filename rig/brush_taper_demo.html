Pose

The noise is baked to a canvas once (feImage), not regenerated live — so this is cheap: boundary/blur/threshold just tweak filter attributes. The stroke is modulated by a ramp, blurred, jittered with the noise, and hard-thresholded to opaque ink, so near the boundary it thins and breaks into dry-brush flecks and past it vanishes. Not opacity — the output is solid ink.
